## Custom perfume mixing (Mix It Into a Spray)

Eligible perfume oils can be hand-blended into a ready-to-wear alcohol spray:

1. Choose bottle size — **50ml or 100ml**.
2. Choose strength — **Eau de Toilette** (4–6 hrs, soft), **Eau de Parfum** (7–9 hrs,
   moderate), or **Extrait / Pure** (10–14 hrs, strong).
3. Made-to-order sprays are hand-blended and dispatched in **3–5 working days**
   (standard in-stock bottles ship within 48 hours).
