Shipping & Delivery

Do you deliver across the whole UAE?add
Yes — we deliver to all seven Emirates, including Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, Fujairah and Umm Al Quwain. Orders above AED 150 ship free.
How much is delivery and how long does it take?add
Delivery is free on orders over AED 150; a small flat fee applies below that. Standard delivery takes 2–4 business days, and in-stock orders are dispatched within 48 hours. Same-day delivery is available in Dubai for orders placed before 2pm.
Can I track my order?add
Absolutely. Once your order ships, we send tracking details by SMS and WhatsApp so you can follow it to your door. You can also message our team any time for a live update.
Do you ship outside the UAE?add
Right now we focus on serving the UAE so we can guarantee fast, reliable delivery and authenticity at every step. For GCC enquiries, message us on WhatsApp and we'll let you know what's possible.

Orders & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?add
We accept Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay and Google Pay, plus cash on delivery across the UAE. You can also split your order interest-free with Tabby or Tamara.
Is cash on delivery really available everywhere?add
Yes — cash on delivery is available UAE-wide. Pay in cash when your order arrives, with no card details needed up front. It's one of the easiest ways to shop with complete peace of mind.
How do Tabby and Tamara work?add
Both let you split your purchase into interest-free instalments. Just choose Tabby or Tamara at checkout, complete a quick approval, and pay the rest over the following weeks — no hidden fees.
Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?add
If your order hasn't shipped yet, we can usually amend or cancel it. Message us on WhatsApp as soon as possible with your order number and we'll sort it out.

Authenticity & Sourcing

Are your fragrances 100% authentic?add
Every single one. We only sell genuine fragrances sourced through verified channels, and each batch is checked before it reaches our shelves. Authenticity is the reason our company exists — it's non-negotiable.
Where do your products come from?add
We combine verified resale of the world's designer and Arabic houses with our own brands — Fragrance Secrets, Secret Scents and the Paris Collection — created here in the UAE. Across 218+ brands and 47+ houses, everything is sourced with traceability in mind.
What are your own brands?add
Fragrance Secrets, Secret Scents and the Paris Collection are our in-house labels — crafted to deliver luxury character and strong performance in UAE weather, at a genuinely fair price.
What if I'm not sure a product is genuine?add
Your confidence matters to us. If you ever have a doubt about a product you received, contact our team on WhatsApp and we'll verify it with you personally — and make it right if anything is off.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?add
We offer hassle-free 7-day returns on all unopened, sealed products. Simply reach out within 7 days of delivery and we'll arrange the rest.
How do I start a return?add
Message us on WhatsApp with your order number and the item you'd like to return. Our team will confirm eligibility and arrange a collection — no complicated forms.
When will I get my refund?add
Once we receive and check your returned item, refunds are processed promptly — typically within a few business days, back to your original payment method or as store credit if you prefer.
Can I return an opened fragrance?add
For hygiene and authenticity reasons, opened or used fragrances can't be returned. If a product arrived damaged or faulty, contact us straight away and we'll replace it or refund you.
