Your Questions, Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between perfume oil and attar?add
Both are alcohol-free oils. Attar traditionally refers to natural distillations — often oud, rose or musk — while perfume oils can also be modern blended compositions. Both wear close to the skin and last for hours.
Which category lasts longest in UAE heat?add
Arabic perfumes, oud-based fragrances and attars perform exceptionally in warm climates thanks to rich, heavy base notes. Look for our 8–12 hour longevity tags, or ask the AI concierge.
Are inspired perfumes authentic?add
Our inspired lines are original compositions that echo the character of iconic scents — always clearly labelled as inspired-by, never sold as the genuine designer original. Our designer and Arabic-house resale is 100% authentic, verified-sourced stock.
Do you offer Cash on Delivery across all categories?add
Yes — Cash on Delivery is available across all seven Emirates, alongside Tabby & Tamara interest-free instalments, Apple Pay, Google Pay and cards.
