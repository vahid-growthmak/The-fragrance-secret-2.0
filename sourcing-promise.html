Sourcing Questions

If it's genuine, why is it cheaper than the mall?add
Because of how we source. Verified-wholesaler channels and our own-brand manufacturing let us price below mall retail without touching authenticity. Lower price here reflects lower overheads and sourcing — not counterfeit product.
Are you an authorised distributor for designer brands?add
No — and we won't claim to be. Designer and Arabic-house fragrances on our site are genuine stock sourced through verified wholesalers and market-authorised channels. Our own brands are exactly that: our own original lines.
How do I know a specific product is genuine?add
Every resale item is factory-sealed, batch-checked and backed by 7-day returns. Want certainty about a specific bottle before you buy? Message our team on WhatsApp (daily 10am–7pm) and a real person will confirm exactly where it comes from.
What does "inspired-by" mean on your own brands?add
Some of our in-house scents are composed to echo the character of iconic fragrances. We label these clearly as inspired-by — they are our original creations, never sold as the genuine designer original.
