In Season Now

Summer 2026: Scents That Beat the Heat

When the mercury climbs past 40°C, heavy fragrance turns cloying. This is the season for luminous citrus, cooling aquatics and airy florals that stay crisp from morning to night.

The Summer Standout

Cool, Clean & Effortless — The Fresh Citrus Revival

Bergamot, neroli, sea salt and vetiver are having a moment. Fragrances like Armaf Club de Nuit and Mancera Cedrat Boise deliver that just-showered brightness that reads elegant in the heat, without ever feeling sharp. Lightweight EDTs and skin-close attars let you re-apply through the day without overwhelming a room.

Trend Spotlight

The Rise of Gourmand Ouds

One trend refuses to fade — the marriage of dessert-sweet gourmand notes with deep, smoky oud.

On the Rise

Coffee, Caramel & Oud — A Modern UAE Signature

Lattafa Khamrah Qahwa and Al Haramain Amber Oud Gold captured something the region can't get enough of: the comfort of gourmand sweetness anchored by resinous, long-wearing oud. Worn lightly it's inviting; worn boldly it's unforgettable — a trend built for evenings, celebrations and cooler nights ahead.
