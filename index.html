Why You'll Love It

Crafted for those who want to stand out, Bouquet of Oud blends deep, resonant oud with the warmth of amber and the sharpness of saffron — a truly magnetic presence that commands attention from the first spray.

The opening is rich and bright with bergamot and saffron, evolving into a heart of Rosa damascena and pure oud resin before settling into a deeply sensual base of amber, musk, and creamy sandalwood.

Perfect For

  • Evening occasions, weddings, and formal events
  • Those who love rich, oriental fragrances
  • Gift giving — comes in premium gift packaging
  • UAE and GCC climate — exceptional projection in heat
